Find My Fam

Find My Fam is web app I made at Deltahacks VI using Django, Python, Javascript, HTML/CSS, and the Google maps API. It is designed to help individuals with Alzheimers and their primary care-takers.

The primary caretaker registers a loved one diagnosed with Alzheimer's and then sets a "safe area" that their loved one would potentially wander into. If that individual exits the boundaries set by the primary caretaker, they will be notified and a path to the user will be given.
